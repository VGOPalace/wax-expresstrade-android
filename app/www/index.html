Deactivate your old TwoFactor

1. Go to https://opskins.com/?loc=store_account#collapseSec
2. Click on "Disable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. You will be prompted to enter your 2FA Token
4. Enter the Token from your old 2FA-App
5. You have now disabled TwoFactor Authentication and need to reenable it
6. Follow the steps below

Activate In-App TwoFactor

1. Go to https://opskins.com/?loc=store_account#collapseSec
2. Click on "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
3. Wait for an Email from OPSkins
4. Input the 2FA Token OPSkins sent you
5. You will now see a page including a QR-Code
6. Click the button below to scan this QR-Code or enter the Secret from below the QR-Code
7. You activated TwoFactor Auth inside our App
8. If you save the Secret below the QR-Code, you can input it in other 2FA-Apps and use them aswell
